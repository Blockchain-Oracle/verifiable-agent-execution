## What it is

The 0G APAC Hackathon is a 2-month builder program running March–May 2026 on HackQuest for AI × Web3 applications built on 0G's modular L1 infrastructure. Sponsored by 0G Labs — the team behind the Aristotle Mainnet (live since Sept 2025), with $290–325M raised and 100+ ecosystem partners. APAC-focused but globally open; solo builders and teams of 1–6. The hackathon is described as a "developer acquisition" program with follow-up pathways into the 0G ecosystem (Apollo AI Accelerator, AIverse marketplace, ecosystem grants).
